How WEBRTC Is Used

What Is It? Why is it Important How is It Used Sources

WebRTC enables video conferencing through platforms which include Zoom and Microsoft Teams and Slack and Google Meet. WebRTC serves multiple industries which include healthcare and surveillance and monitoring and internet of things applications.

Telehealth services use WebRTC to let doctors perform virtual office visits with patients through web browser interfaces. WebRTC functions as a security camera browser connection tool for home and business surveillance systems.

WebRTC enables instructors to establish direct connections with their students for online educational purposes. WebRTC serves as the fundamental connection system which enables instructors to teach students through online educational platforms.
