Why WEBRTC Are Important

What Is It? Why is it Important How is It Used Sources

WebRTC functions as the fundamental technology which enables real-time voice and video communications through web pages and applications. All current browsers support WebRTC technology which developers can use to create real-time voice and video communications through web pages and applications

The technology exists as a standard feature in all major platforms through native client support. The video conferencing platforms Google Meet and Slack and Microsoft Teams operate through WebRTC technology. WebRTC technology enables telehealth services to provide video appointments between patients and doctors.

The implementation of WebRTC enables browsers to establish dependable low-latency connections which make human collaboration through voice and video communication possible. The complete set of features in WebRTC enables developers to create real-time communication functions which they can integrate into their current applications through a fast and straightforward process.
