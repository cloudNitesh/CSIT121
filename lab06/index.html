Accessibility Evaluation: Marvel Movies Website

1. What is the URL of the website?

https://www.marvel.com

2. What is the name of the website?

The name of the website is Marvel Movies.

3. Who is the site's target audience?

The site targets Marvel fans, movie enthusiasts who are interested in upcoming releases, trailers, and cinematic news related to the Marvel Universe.

4. How is the site organized?

The site is organized into categories such as Movies, TV Shows, Comics, and Games. It uses a top navigation bar and SlideShow Featuring different Items. Then it list different articles and sections for easy access to content. In footer there are links to social media and additional resources.

5. Which CRAP Design Principle does the site use?

The site effectively uses the Contrast principle by employing a dark background with bright, colorful images and text to make important elements stand out. It also uses Repetition through consistent fonts, colors, and button styles across different pages to create a cohesive look and feel.

6. What is the Audit Score according to the Accessibility Checker?

The Audit Score for the Marvel Movies website is 41 out of 100.

7. What is the site's effectiveness?

The site is effective in delivering information about Marvel movies. Users can easily access trailers, release dates, and latest details are showing at top.

8. What is the site's efficiency?

The site loads quickly and allows users to have look at latest deatil about coming soon movies, and trailers with minimal clicks.

9. How is the engagement?

The engagement level is high, it uses dynamic visuals, trailers, and interactive hover effects. The site matches its entertainment purpose well, making browsing exciting and visually appealing.

10. Recommendation for Improvement

To improve accessibility, Marvel can add alternative text to images for screen readers. Additionally, They can ensure that all interactive elements are keyboard navigable for users with disabilities.
